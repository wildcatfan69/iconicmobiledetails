About Us

I am an engineering graduate from the UK who has always had an affinity for cars, particularly an obsession with how clean people could get their cars. I could not wait until the day I was 16 to get my first car and keep it clean. My hobby grew into a passion. Over the next 10 years, I researched the best products, practices, and techniques to preserve paint and restore the factory shine. My engineering background plays a role as well, studying the reactions and complements of different products and benchmarking their success to provide you, the customer, with the best all-around detailing process in Kentucky.
